# Mr. Tan Advisor Dialogue Script

Use this on the live meeting page between Sarah Lim and Mr. Tan Wee Seng.

The goal is to clearly trigger six capabilities:

1. personal memory recall
2. unresolved concern detection
3. suggested advisor question
4. relationship and goal context
5. best partner recommendation
6. promised follow-up and memory capture

## Full Demo Dialogue

### Capability 1: Personal Memory Recall

Sarah:
"Hi Mr. Tan, good to see you again. Before we start, congratulations again on Jia En getting into NUS. That must be a proud moment for the family."

Mr. Tan:
"Thank you, Sarah. Yes, the whole family is very happy. It also made me think more seriously about making sure everything is properly arranged for them."

Expected app signal:

- family milestone prompt or captured memory about Jia En and NUS

### Capability 2: Unresolved Concern Detection

Sarah:
"That makes sense. Last time, you mentioned that the will update was still unresolved and that it felt hard to start. Is that still the main thing on your mind?"

Mr. Tan:
"Yes. I know I should update the will, but I am not sure what documents I need or who I should speak to. I have been putting it off."

Sarah:
"Understood. So the concern is not whether you want to do it, but how to get started without making it complicated."

Expected app signal:

- suggested follow-up about what is blocking the will update
- captured memory that will planning remains open

### Capability 3: Suggested Advisor Question

Pause here and open the `Ask` tab if it appears.

Expected suggested question:

- "Ask what would make the will update easier to start."
- "Ask whether a specialist introduction would reduce the friction."

Sarah:
"What would make this easier to start: a simple checklist, an introduction to a specialist, or setting a first appointment?"

Mr. Tan:
"A specialist introduction would help most. I think I just need someone trusted to guide me through the documents."

Expected app signal:

- advisor-only suggested question
- relevant memory lookup around estate planning or referral need

### Capability 4: Relationship And Goal Context

Mr. Tan:
"Exactly. I want it to be simple. I also do not want my wife or Jia En to be confused later."

Sarah:
"That matches what you shared previously about wanting a smooth family transition with low conflict. Would it help if I introduced you to someone who focuses on estate planning?"

Expected app signal:

- relevant memory about smooth family transition
- relationship context involving wife and Jia En

### Capability 5: Best Partner Recommendation

Mr. Tan:
"Yes, that would help. Do you still know the estate planning specialist you mentioned?"

Sarah:
"Yes. Let me check the best match from my specialist network. Evelyn Ng is the recommended partner here. She works on estate planning and can help you understand the will update process."

Mr. Tan:
"Please introduce me to Evelyn. I would prefer to speak with someone trusted."

Sarah:
"I can do that. I will draft a warm introduction to Evelyn after this meeting."

Expected app signal:

- one direct best partner card for Evelyn Ng
- captured referral opportunity or introduction action

### Capability 6: Follow-Up Capture And Review

Mr. Tan:
"Thank you. Also, you mentioned an estate planning guide before. Could you send that too?"

Sarah:
"Yes, I will send the estate planning guide today. The most useful sections for you will be the will update checklist and the family transition notes."

Mr. Tan:
"That sounds good. I also want to review whether my current protection policy still makes sense, but maybe we can start with the will first."

Sarah:
"Good approach. We will make the will update the first priority, then review the protection policy once that next step is moving."

Mr. Tan:
"Yes, that feels manageable."

Sarah:
"Let me confirm the next steps. I will send you the estate planning guide, draft the introduction to Evelyn Ng, and create a reminder to revisit the protection policy after the will planning step is underway."

Mr. Tan:
"Perfect. Thank you, Sarah."

Expected app signal:

- follow-up action to send the estate planning guide
- follow-up action to draft the Evelyn Ng introduction
- possible reminder to revisit protection policy
- post-meeting review items awaiting advisor approval
